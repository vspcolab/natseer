About Natseer

Natseer aims to bridge the gap between organic food sources and conscious consumers by integrating B2B and B2C supply chains for sustainable, high-quality, and science-backed meal solutions. Through 101 Y-Diet Meal Packages, it caters to hospitals, cloud kitchens, yoga centers, fitness centers, and individuals, ensuring holistic nutrition for all.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the most trusted supplier of organic and natural food raw materials, fostering holistic wellness through clean, sustainable, and science-backed nutrition solutions.

Mission

  • To provide high-quality organic and natural ingredients to businesses and consumers.
  • To develop science-based, tradition-rooted meal solutions (Y-Diet 101 Modules).
  • To support wellness-focused establishments like hospitals, yoga centers, and cloud kitchens.
  • To create a seamless supply chain for organic raw materials and meal kits.
  • To educate and empower consumers to make informed dietary choices.

Business Model

B2B (Business-to-Business) Model

Natseer supplies organic raw materials and meal package kits (Y-Diet 101 Modules) to various industries.

Target B2B Customers

  1. Cloud Kitchens – Ready-to-cook meal packages for health-focused meal delivery.
  2. Hospitals & Wellness Clinics – Diet-based meal packages for patient nutrition.
  3. Restaurants & Cafeterias – Specialty diet offerings for health-conscious consumers.
  4. Supermarkets & Organic Stores – Retail distribution of Y-Diet meal kits.
  5. Yoga Centers & Retreats – Holistic meal plans aligned with yogic principles.
  6. Fitness Centers & Gyms – Nutrient-rich meal plans for athletic and wellness diets.
  7. Educational Institutions & Hostels – Healthy, balanced meal options.
  8. Corporate Canteens – Organic meal solutions for office employees.
  9. Luxury Resorts & Hotels – Premium organic meal kits for guests.
  10. Ayurvedic Clinics & Holistic Health Centers – Specialty diets aligned with Ayurveda.

B2C (Business-to-Consumer) Model

Natseer offers personalized Y-Diet meal kits directly to consumers through various channels.

Target B2C Customers

  1. Health Enthusiasts & Fitness Seekers – Individuals looking for clean, organic nutrition.
  2. People with Dietary Restrictions – Gluten-free, dairy-free, or specific diet requirements.
  3. Yoga & Meditation Practitioners – Consumers seeking sattvic meals.
  4. Working Professionals – Ready-to-eat or easy-prep meal kits.
  5. Pregnant Women & Postpartum Mothers – Special nutrition kits.
  6. Elderly Individuals – Digestive-friendly organic food solutions.
  7. Children & Students – Brain-boosting meal packages.
  8. Vegan & Plant-Based Diet Followers – Sustainable meal solutions.
  9. Ayurvedic & Holistic Health Consumers – Custom herbal-infused diets.
  10. Special Diet Followers – Customized high-protein/low-carb options.

Strategies

B2B Strategies

  • Bulk Distribution Model: Partner with retailers and large-scale suppliers for bulk orders.
  • Subscription Model for Businesses: Monthly supply contracts for hospitals, restaurants, and yoga centers.
  • White Labeling: Offer private labeling services for supermarkets and specialty stores.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Collaborate with fitness centers, wellness brands, and corporate canteens.
  • Supply Chain Optimization: Efficient farm-to-business logistics for cost-effectiveness.

B2C Strategies

  • Direct-to-Consumer E-commerce: Sell Y-Diet kits via the website and online marketplaces.
  • Subscription Plans: Personalized diet meal plans with weekly/monthly delivery.
  • Retail Presence: Set up in organic grocery stores and supermarkets.
  • Influencer & Community Marketing: Partner with fitness coaches, nutritionists, and health bloggers.
  • RAG - Science-Back-Powered Meal Recommendations: Personalized diet plans based on health analysis.

Operational Structure

B2B Operations Departments

  1. Sourcing & Procurement – Organic raw material acquisition from certified farms.
  2. Quality Assurance & Compliance – Ensuring food safety and organic certification.
  3. Production & Packaging – Processing and packaging of meal kits.
  4. Sales & Business Development – Expanding partnerships with B2B customers.
  5. Logistics & Supply Chain – Managing inventory and timely delivery to businesses.
  6. Customer Support & B2B Relationship Management – Handling bulk order queries and support.

B2C Operations Departments

  1. Product Development – Designing personalized Y-Diet meal packages.
  2. E-commerce & Retail Management – Handling online orders and retail distribution.
  3. Marketing & Branding – Digital marketing, social media, and customer engagement.
  4. Subscription & Customer Service – Managing personalized diet plans and feedback.
  5. Logistics & Home Delivery – Ensuring efficient last-mile delivery for B2C orders.
